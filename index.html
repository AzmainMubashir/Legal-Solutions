The Legal Practice Area

Legal practice involves applying laws, representing clients, and ensuring justice through court proceedings, legal advice, and documentation.

Business Law

Business law governs commercial interactions, covering contracts, corporate governance, intellectual property, employment, and consumer protection.

Criminal Law

Criminal law defines crimes, regulates the apprehension, charging, and trial of suspects, and fixes penalties for convicted offenders.

Child Support

Child support involves court-ordered payments by a noncustodial parent to support their minor child’s living expenses.

Education Law

Education law governs the rights of students, teachers, and schools, covering public and private education systems and special education.

Divorce Law

Divorce law governs the legal dissolution of marriage, addressing property division, spousal support, child custody, and support.

Tax Law

Tax law governs the assessment, collection, and payment of taxes by individuals and entities, based on legal regulations.
